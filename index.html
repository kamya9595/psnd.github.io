About the College

Pandit Shrinivas Dwivedi Mahavidyalaya is a degree college located in Arabgi, Sayadraja, Chandauli, Uttar Pradesh. The college is affiliated with Mahatma Gandhi Kashi Vidyapeeth, Varanasi, and is committed to providing quality higher education to students from rural and surrounding areas.

Courses Offered

  • Bachelor of Arts (B.A.)
  • Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)
